Policies & Membership

The fine print, made plain.

Everything about how memberships, cancellations, and studio visits work.

Membership Terms

  • Founding Member Unlimited — $80/mo, limited to the first 100 members.
  • Membership includes one use per day of each modality (cold plunge, infrared sauna, and compression therapy).
  • No long-term contracts. Memberships are month-to-month.

Drop-In Pricing

  • Single modality (cold plunge, sauna, or compression): $15/visit
  • Full circuit (all three modalities): $40/visit

Cancellations & Refunds

Membership cancellations remain active through the end of the current billing cycle. No refunds or proration are issued for partial months.

There is no fee for cancelling an appointment. However, if you have not arrived within 10 minutes of your scheduled appointment time, your spot may be given to the next available client.

Health & Eligibility

ReLife Recovery welcomes clients ages 16 and up. If you're under 18, a parent or legal guardian must sign your liability waiver before your first visit.

Please consult a physician before your first session if you have a cardiovascular condition, are pregnant, or have uncontrolled high blood pressure — cold plunge and infrared sauna use may not be safe for these conditions.

A liability waiver is required once, at your first visit — you won't need to re-sign on future visits.

Towel & Facility Policy

Clients are welcome to bring their own towel, or rent one in-studio for $2/use. Please allow staff to direct you on modality-specific setup and safety guidance during your first visit.

Governing Law
